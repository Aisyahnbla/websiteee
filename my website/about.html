Biodata

The life of a woman who tries to strengthen herself in the face of challenges.Hello,my name is Siti Aisyahnabila Binti Mohd Ghazali or better known as"Aisyah".I am 21 years old and come from Pasir Mas,Kelantan.Raised in a modest family of 11 siblings and I am the youngest.I have 7 brothers and 3 sisters. I was born at Hospital Besar Kota Bharu and grew up in the village next to my father because my mother was from Kota Bharu.My father died in 2013 but alhamdulillah until this day my mother is still on my side.My hobby is to read novels and listen to music,it's not limited to just one genre but a variety. I also like to try new menus in cooking and sometimes I bake cakes. Everything follows my time and craft at any given time.Although I was the youngest child, I was raised by brothers who taught me so much about life that I was able to go out for money without expecting money from my family.
